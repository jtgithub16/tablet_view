Spicy Fried Chicken Sandwich

Origin: American

Source: Original Recipe

Category: Main Dish

This is a classic dish based off of a Chic-fil-a recipe! I made it myself, enjoy! - Johann Tsegay

Recipe Ingredients

Chicken Prep:

  • 1.5lb Chicken Thigh
  • 2 tbsp garlic powder
  • 2 tbsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tbsp cumin
  • 1 tbsp black pepper
  • dash/pinch of salt
  • 3 cups of flour
  • 3 tbsp oil
  • 1 tbsp hot sauce

Marinade

  • 1 cup hot sauce
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • Dash of salt
  • 4 tbsp melted butter

Bun & Toppings

  • 4 hamburger buns
  • Crinkled Pickles
  • Tomatoes
  • Chipotle Mayo
  • Lettuce

Recipe Steps

  1. Wash your chicken and leave it to rest in a bowl
  2. Begin working on your marinade. In a medium/large bowl, combine your hot sauce, eggs, black pepper, butter, and salt one-by-one. Mix until fully dissolved
  3. In a separate bowl, combine flour, garlic powder, paprika, cumin, black pepper, salt together. Whisk thoroughly.
  4. Prep your chicken thigh by poking holes with a fork across its surface on both sides. Place your chicken into the marinade mix and let rest for 1-2h. (if speed is needed, can do a quick soak of 10-15min)
  5. Place your marinated chicken into flour mixture and toss. Place back in marinade and redcoat. Repeat 2-3 times.
  6. Prepare your vegetables and set them aside.
  7. Grab a deep pan and add your oil on medium-high heat. Fry in oil for 4-5min on each side.
  8. Remove chicken thigh and toss in 1tbsp hot sauce before plating.
  9. Combine the buns, toppings, and chicken and serve!

Crunchwrap Supreme

The notorious Crunchwrap Supreme created by the infamous Taco Bell.

Taco Bell is the best fastfood out there. When you're hungry it hits the spot constanlty bursting with flavor.

Many people might not favor taco bell because of how unhealthy it is but there are many taco bell chains out there than actual mexican places.

Recipe Ingredients

  • Tortilla
  • Ground Beef
  • Circular Taco Shell
  • Cheese
  • Lettuce
  • Sour Cream
  • Melted Cheese
  • Diced Tomatoes
  • Taco Bell Seasoning
  • Cheese Sauce

Steps

  1. Make the ground beef with taco seasoning, season it well with spices and Taco Bell spices from Target
  2. Lay the tortilla on the grill and put cheese sauce on top of it, smooth it out in a circular motion, and be generous with the amount
  3. Next, put the circular crunch taco shell on top of it while adding a generous amount of sour cream on it, don't be shy!
  4. Next add your seasoned to ground beef onto the tortilla
  5. Finally, add your diced tomatoes, lettuce, and cheese.
  6. Wrap your Crunchwrap pentagon style and then grill it on a hot pan for about 4 minutes on medium heat, both sides
  7. Take it off the pan and then cut it in half and you have your Crunchwrap supreme

Tomato and Egg

Origin: China

Source: Own Recipe

Category: Main Dish

Tomato and eggs is a hot dish which originated from China. It is a simple and fast dish to make, but it has good nutritional value.
It is a very satisfying food to eat and popularly served with rice and topped with green onions.

Recipe Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Salt
  • Sugar
  • Cooking oil
  • Tomatoes

Steps

  1. Crack eggs into a bowl and mix until scrambled
  2. Cut tomatoes into pieces around the size of your thumb
  3. Oil a hot pan and cook the eggs until satisfied then remove the eggs
  4. Re-oil the pan and cook the tomatoes until they loosen
  5. Combine eggs and tomatoes and season with salt and sugar
